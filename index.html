About

The Remote Healthcare Society (RHS) is a multidisciplinary non-profit organization dedicated to the research and clinical application of evidence based remote and wireless monitoring solutions across the global continuum of care.

Our Mission

The mission of the society is to optimize quality of life, independence, and health maintenance through the deployment of remote medical technology by utilizing a collaborative care model, so as to bring together global health care providers, scientists, engineers, private payers, self-insured employers, unions, and most importantly, the individual patient and person.

Committees

RHS Committees, Subcommittees, Task-Forces, and Alliance Councils are dedicated to advocating for the expansion of global clinical adoption of remote and wireless monitoring solution on behalf of RHS members.
